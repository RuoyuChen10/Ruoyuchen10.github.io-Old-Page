Welcome

Posted on 2021-02-19 Edited on 2021-09-24

I’m Ruoyu Chen (陈若愚), a Ph.D. student in Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS), advised by Xiaochun Cao (Professor at IIE, CAS). My research interests include Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision. My recent research works mainly focus on Explainable AI, Disentangle, Few-shot Learning, which applications in Human Face, Object Detection. I received my B.E. degree from School of Control Engineering, Northeastern University. During my undergraduate years, my main research interests are the application of artificial intelligence and image processing in agriculture and biomedical engineering, cooperate with Yuliang Zhao (Associate professor at NEU), Yongliang Yang (Associate professor at SIA, CAS) and Wen Jung Li (Chair Professor at CityU, IEEE Fellow).
